Common sawaal — seedhe jawab
1000 sqft ghar banane mein kitna kharcha?
Metro city mein standard quality ke liye approximately ₹19–21 lakh (₹1,900–2,100/sqft). Tier-2 city mein yahi ₹14–16 lakh mein ho sakta hai. Exact estimate ke liye upar ka construction calculator use karein.
Contractor ka quote sahi hai ya nahi — kaise pata karein?
Apne city aur quality ke hisaab se per sqft rate compare karein. Metro mein standard quality: ₹1,900–2,100/sqft fair hai. Isse 20% zyada aa raha hai toh 2–3 aur quotes lein. Hamare Quote Checker se seedha check karein.
2 BHK interior kitne mein hota hai?
Metro city mein 2BHK ka complete interior: Basic ₹3.5–5 lakh | Standard ₹7–10 lakh | Premium ₹12–18 lakh. Room-wise exact estimate ke liye Interior Estimator try karein.
50 lakh ke home loan ki EMI kitni hogi?
8.5% rate, 20 saal tenure par monthly EMI approximately ₹43,391. Tenure 10 saal rakhein toh EMI ₹61,993 hogi — lekin total interest ₹30 lakh kam lagega. EMI Calculator mein apne figures daalen.
