Frequently Asked Questions

What is the construction cost per sqft in Mumbai in 2026?+

In Mumbai, construction costs range from ₹1,400/sqft (Economy) to ₹3,200/sqft (Premium) in 2026. Standard quality construction typically costs ₹1,900–2,100/sqft. South Mumbai and Bandra commands 15–20% premium over suburban rates.

Why is construction so expensive in Mumbai?+

Mumbai's costs are driven by: highest skilled labour wages in India (masons ₹800–1,100/day), expensive transport due to island city geography and congestion, BMC's high development charges, and stricter structural requirements for coastal and high-rise buildings.

Is Navi Mumbai cheaper than Mumbai to build in?+

Yes. Navi Mumbai (NMMC) is 10–15% cheaper than Mumbai suburbs. Standard quality runs ₹1,700–1,950/sqft vs ₹1,900–2,100 in Mumbai. NMMC approval fees are also lower than BMC.

What are cement and steel prices in Mumbai Q1 2026?+

OPC 53 cement: ₹370–420/bag. TMT steel Fe500: ₹62,000–68,000/MT — 5–8% higher than inland Maharashtra due to logistics. Lock in material prices before structural work starts.

How long does BMC building approval take?+

BMC requires IOD + CC — takes 3–8 months for complete applications per DCR 2034 norms. Budget ₹1–3 lakh for professional and statutory fees. Use a licensed architect familiar with BMC's iDCPR portal.
