Frequently Asked Questions

What is the construction cost per sqft in Pune in 2026?+

In Pune, construction costs range from ₹1,200/sqft (Economy) to ₹2,400/sqft (Premium) in 2026. Standard quality construction typically costs ₹1,650–1,850/sqft. Pune is 10–15% cheaper than Mumbai for equivalent quality construction, making it one of the best value markets in Maharashtra.

What is the difference between PMC and PCMC jurisdiction in Pune?+

PMC (Pune Municipal Corporation) covers the core Pune city area including Kothrud, Aundh, Baner, Shivajinagar, Kharadi, and Hadapsar. PCMC (Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation) covers the industrial areas of Pimpri, Chinchwad, Akurdi, and Nigdi. PCMC areas typically have lower development charges than PMC. Hinjewadi falls in PMRDA (Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority) jurisdiction.

Why is Pune construction cheaper than Mumbai?+

Pune has lower construction costs than Mumbai due to: lower labour wages (masons earn ₹650–900/day vs ₹800–1,100/day in Mumbai), shorter material transport distances from major suppliers, lower municipal development charges vs BMC, and a competitive contractor market with many skilled workers. Pune also has fewer geographic constraints (no island city issues).

What are current material prices in Pune 2026?+

In Pune as of early 2026: OPC 53 cement costs ₹350–390/bag (ACC, Ultratech; good availability from nearby Nanded plants). TMT steel Fe500 costs ₹59,000–65,000/MT. AAC blocks cost ₹38–48/block. M-sand is widely available and preferred over river sand. Pune's proximity to Maharashtra's cement belt keeps material costs competitive.

How long does PMC building plan approval take in Pune?+

PMC building plan approval for residential projects typically takes 2–4 months if documents are complete and the design complies with Pune's DP (Development Plan) norms. PCMC approval is often faster at 1.5–3 months. PMRDA approvals for peripheral areas take 3–6 months. The PMC has an online portal (iSarita) that has improved transparency and reduced timelines.
