Frequently Asked Questions

What is the construction cost per sqft in Ahmedabad in 2026?+

Ahmedabad construction costs range from ₹1,150/sqft (Economy) to ₹2,300/sqft (Premium) in 2026. Standard quality is ₹1,600–1,800/sqft — the lowest of any major Indian metro.

Why is Ahmedabad construction cheaper than other metros?+

Ahmedabad has: lowest material costs in India (cement ₹335–375/bag, steel ₹58,000–63,000/MT from GIDC dealers), lowest mason wages (₹550–780/day), well-developed contractor ecosystem, and planned TP scheme plots with utilities pre-planned.

What is the TP scheme system in Ahmedabad?+

The Town Planning (TP) scheme — managed by AUDA — is a land readjustment mechanism where plotted developments are pre-planned with roads and utilities. Most residential plots in Ahmedabad are in TP schemes, simplifying approval and reducing development charges.

How do AUDA and AMC approvals differ?+

AMC covers core Ahmedabad city. AUDA covers surrounding areas including Bopal and Chandkheda. AUDA approvals processed online via the AUDA portal — 2–4 months. Development charges slightly lower in AUDA vs AMC zones.

What are material prices in Ahmedabad Q1 2026?+

OPC 53 cement ₹335–375/bag — India's lowest. TMT steel Fe500 ₹58,000–63,000/MT from GSFC/GIDC dealers. M-sand/crush sand ₹30–42/sqft. Mason wages ₹550–780/day.
